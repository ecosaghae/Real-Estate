"FREE BUILDING"

  • Purchase price of 2 unit: $200,000
  • Monthly mortgage: $1000
  • Each unit pays $900/month. $1800 monthly rental income
  • Rents cover mortgage ($1000) + provide additional $800/month in passive income








POWER OF EQUITY

  • Purchase price of 2 unit: $200,000
  • Down Payment: $10,000(5%)
  • $10,000 allowed you to acquire $200,000 asset

    • If purchasing stocks, $10,000 would give you $10,000 worth of stocks

  • Can refinance and capture equity from initial $10,000 down payment

    • Renewable source of capital
